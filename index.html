about us

Feed The Poor Foundation

Feed The poor Foundation, is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization whose mission is to alleviate poverty and hunger in Kolkata. We work to feed, empower, cloth, and provide basic amenities such as food, water, electricity, housing, and healthcare to the poorest of Kolkata.
